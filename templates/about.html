About Approachable Art

Welcome to Approachable Art! This web app was created to aid the average joe in learning about the wonderful world of art and its history. Browsing our galleries, you will be taken on an adventure from France in the Baroque period to Italy in the Renaissance period and beyond. Whether you’re learning for pleasure or cramming to impress your boss, we have the information for you.

Approachable Art is the brainchild of Olivia Coghe, a student at Penn State Behrend. Olivia is a double major in Digital Media, Art, and Technology as well as Arts Administration. Approachable Art has been a way for her to combine her various passions including art, programming, and education. She firmly believes that no art should be out of reach and that everyone should have the opportunity to enjoy art’s many beauties and stories.

Olivia personally curates each gallery with information and accompanying photos. She encourages the breaking down of the seemingly foreign discourse we often encounter in the art world.

Mission

The aim of this project is to provide simple, accessible art lessons that quickly and efficiently teach the main concepts of a given topic to our aspiring learners.

How to Use

Visitors should create a profile and log in to enter the site and have access to all the tools on the website. Then visitors can look through the wall feed and click on something that catches their eye. They can click on the read more tab to be taken to a page detailing that subject with picture or video, as well as external links. If visitors have a hard time knowing where to start, they can click the Random! button and be taken to a randomly selected topic to learn about. After learning about a specific movement or author, the visitor should move to the gallery page and search for key terms from that lesson. A carousel of images regarding the key terms will appear to be looked through. Eventually, on the dashboard tab, found under profile, visitors can access their personal profiles, participate in forums, and personalize their homepage based off of interests.

Future Updates

The future steps of Approachable Art will include adding the ability for personalization of profile pages as well as a social sharing aspect. The hope is that this website will serve as a community to those who want to learn. Community members will be able to interact with one another, talk about what art means or challenge ideas of art history. It will serve as a place to share what one is learning about and comment on what art piece is their favorite. Eventually, people can check into different states or cities and be able to see art galleries, museums, and pieces nearby. The mission is to create an approachable space, a social learning environment. These parts of the application will only be accessible through becoming a member and creating a login. Lastly, galleries will have more information added to them, as well as the addition of new topics.

Creation

This application was created using Python, HTML, and jQuery and for the purpose of learning how to create a functional website that benefits society in some way. To learn more about the technical creation of this app, visit this google slideshow or Olivia's Github profile.
